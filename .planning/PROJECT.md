# Project: NagrikAI (Civic Translator Upgrade)

## Vision
To bridge the gap between complex civic bureaucracy and the "next billion users" through a high-trust, AI-powered document simplification tool that respects rural digital constraints.

## Guiding Principles
1. **Frictionless Entry**: No login required. Users can start immediately in "Visitor Mode."
2. **Visual-First UI**: Use concrete iconography and imagery over dense text to aid comprehension across literacy levels.
3. **Voice-Centric**: Every summary and interaction has an accompanying voice-over option.
4. **High-Trust Branding**: Colors and typography should feel authoritative yet approachable.

## Brand Identity (Vishwa)
- **Primary Color**: Deep Indigo (#1A237E) - Represents trust, authority, and stability.
- **Accent Color**: Saffron (#FF9800) - Represents energy, warmth, and action.
- **Typography**: Inter (Modern, highly legible) for technical text; Outfit for headings (Premium, accessible).
- **Iconography**: Minimalist, flat icons with labels.

## Target Audience
- Rural citizens in India.
- Users with varying levels of literacy and English proficiency.
- NGO ground workers assisting citizens.
